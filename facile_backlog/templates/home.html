What is backlogman

Backlogman is targeted to help managing your SCRUM backlogs.

As a PRODUCT OWNER, it will simplify your backlogs management by having an easy way to order user stories

As a TEAM MEMBER, it will enhance user stories visibility and help understanding priorities in a project.

Agility in mind

Backlogman is not another tool to track time and team productivity, it simply focus on managing the backlog and add capability to export and print those backlogs in various ways.

Backlogman was developed with agility in mind. Its main target is software development but it may be used in other domain, feel free to experiment...

If you are new in agile methodologies, let's start by reading the agile manifesto. then, let's the journey begin, register to backlogman...
